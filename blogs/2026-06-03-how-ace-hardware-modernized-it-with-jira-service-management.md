The 102-year-old Ace Hardware co-op replaced its outdated email-based ticketing system with Jira Service Management across 5,200 stores, implementing extensive integrations with Salesforce, Azure DevOps, and SolarWinds. The phased rollout prioritized clear requirements, strategic partnerships, and automation to eliminate manual work while enabling real-time visibility across IT operations.
