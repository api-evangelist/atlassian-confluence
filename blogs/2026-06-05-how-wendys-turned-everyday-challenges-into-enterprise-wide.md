Wendy's expanded its Atlassian platform from 147 to nearly 1,000 users in less than two years by solving real business problems across IT and operations teams. The transformation eliminated spreadsheet-heavy processes through five key use cases, including capital labor tracking, learning management system implementation, restaurant operations task management, sustainability program visibility, and quality assurance issue resolution. Success relied on relationship-building, change management frameworks like "Switch," and focusing on practical solutions rather than feature adoption.
