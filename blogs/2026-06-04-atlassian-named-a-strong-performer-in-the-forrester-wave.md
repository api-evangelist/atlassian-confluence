Atlassian earned Strong Performer status in Forrester's Q2 2026 Strategic Portfolio Management evaluation, scoring 5 out of 5 on Vision and Innovation. The company attributes this recognition to its Strategy Collection and AI-powered approach to closing the gap between strategic priorities and executable work, which research shows remains a major enterprise challenge.
